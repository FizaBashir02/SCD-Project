Project

SoundCraft is a beginner-friendly web-based Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) that allows users to record, mix, and export audio tracks directly in the browser. It features a simple, intuitive interface with a mini DAW, waveform visualization, timeline controls, and track management. Users can also browse images, play audio files, and use a contact form. The project includes multi-page navigation, light/dark theme support, and a responsive design for desktop and mobile devices.
